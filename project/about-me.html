Why Do this Site

I love painting myself! I am not really good at it, but I have found it to be relaxing and fun to do both alone and with other people. It can really create nice moments, and it is fun to see other people’s creative thinking and way of portraying their ideas. I wanted to create a website to demonstrate some of my friend’s art to show off what they have worked on!

Who I Am

My name is Daniel Lugo and I am 22 years old. I am a senior in the college of Arts and Sciences studying Biotechnology at Syracuse University. I love playing tennis, cooking, watching movies, hanging out with friends and family, and learning new things! My favorite foods are Italian and Asian food. I am from the Bronx in New York City where I went to High School and played in the tennis team.

I am very interested in machine learning/Artificial intelligence, back-end engineering, embedded software engineering, and medical software engineering. I hope to learn more about cooking and travel the world!
